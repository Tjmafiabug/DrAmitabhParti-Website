Professional Trajectory

Over fifteen years of clinical practice focused on complex diagnostic challenges. Formerly a research fellow at the Institute for Preventive Medicine, Julian has pioneered internal protocols for managing multi-systemic chronic inflammation in geriatric populations. His career is marked by a commitment to the "slow medicine" movement, prioritizing patient narrative alongside clinical data.
