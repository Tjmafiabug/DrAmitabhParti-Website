01.

Medical Disclaimer

Information provided on this platform is for educational and informational purposes only.

The content authored by Dr. Amitabh Parti within 'Dr. Clinical Record' does not constitute professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Medical knowledge is constantly evolving; while we strive for accuracy, clinical protocols may change based on new research.

Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you have read on this digital record. In case of a medical emergency, contact your local healthcare provider or emergency services immediately.

Case studies presented are anonymized and intended for peer-to-peer educational discourse. They should not be used as a self-diagnostic tool by patients.

02.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. This policy outlines how we handle the minimal data we collect when you interact with this journal. We believe in "Data Minimalism"—collecting only what is necessary to deliver the content you request.

Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Email addresses provided for correspondence are used solely for the intended communication.

Cookies

We use essential technical cookies to ensure the stability of the broadsheet layout. No tracking pixels or third-party advertising cookies are deployed.

We adhere to global data protection standards, ensuring that your digital footprint on this clinical record remains private and secure.

03.

NMC Ethics & Compliance

This digital presence is maintained in strict accordance with the **National Medical Commission (NMC)** Code of Ethics. Dr. Amitabh Parti ensures that all clinical commentary is professional, non-promotional, and focused on scientific advancement.

  • /01 No solicitation or advertisement of medical services.
  • /02 Evidence-based clinical findings only.
  • /03 Complete transparency regarding clinical affiliations.
