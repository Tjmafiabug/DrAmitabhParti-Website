About

A physician who believes
precision is an act of respect.

Dr. Parti writes on internal medicine, public health, and the discipline of clinical attention. Over 38 years at the bedside — and now as Senior Director at Fortis Memorial Research Institute, Gurgaon — he has built a practice grounded in rigour, patience, and the conviction that listening is the primary diagnostic instrument.
