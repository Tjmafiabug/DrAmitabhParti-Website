Dr. Parti writes on internal medicine, public health, and the discipline of clinical attention. Over 38 years at the bedside — and now as Senior Director at Fortis Memorial Research Institute, Gurgaon — he has built a practice grounded in rigour, patience, and the conviction that listening is the primary diagnostic instrument.

Read the full biography

Dr. Amitabh Parti is Senior Director and Unit Head of Internal Medicine at Fortis Memorial Research Institute, Gurgaon — one of India's foremost tertiary care hospitals. He has been in clinical practice for 38 years and holds an MD in Internal Medicine from the University of Punjab, where he also completed his MBBS with a Gold Medal.

His practice is grounded in general internal medicine, with particular depth in infectious disease, hypertension, diabetes management, and thyroid disorders. He is an officially empanelled visiting physician to several major corporate health programmes, including Engineers India Limited, the University of Delhi, and Indus Towers Limited.

Alongside his clinical work, Dr. Parti writes on internal medicine, public health, and the discipline of clinical attention. His essays are addressed to the lay reader: they do not assume a medical background, but they do assume a reader willing to think carefully. The concerns that animate his writing — listening, patience, the limits of certainty — are the same concerns that have shaped his clinical practice over nearly four decades.

This site is a professional home: a place for his writing, his credentials, and a record of his clinical interests. It is not a channel for clinical consultation. For patient care, please contact Fortis Memorial through their official appointment systems.

— Dr. Amitabh Parti
